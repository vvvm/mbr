F.A.Q.

Do premium Mobirise extensions and themes require a yearly license renewal?

No, the license for all purchased extensions is FOR LIFE and valid for a LIFETIME with your account. Additionally, the license includes 1 year of complimentary support and free updates.

I see the “auto-renewal” option on the order page. What is this?

The lifetime license includes 1 year of free updates and support. If you tick the "auto-renewal" checkbox, your support and update period will automatically be renewed at a discounted rate after a year. Leave the "auto-renewal" unchecked if you don't need this or plan to renew manually. 

Will my extensions stop working after a year? What happens when the year of free upgrades/support ends?

The purchased extensions and themes will remain completely functional. The only consequence of not renewing is that you will miss out on product updates and you will no longer receive customer support.
